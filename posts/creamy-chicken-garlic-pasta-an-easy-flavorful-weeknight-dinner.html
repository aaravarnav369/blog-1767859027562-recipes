Creamy Chicken Garlic Pasta: An Easy & Flavorful Weeknight Dinner

Pasta Recipes 1/9/2026

Craving a delicious and easy dinner that comes together in minutes? Look no further than this Creamy Chicken Garlic Pasta! This recipe is a true weeknight winner, combining tender chicken, flavorful garlic, and a luscious creamy sauce that coats every strand of pasta. It's a guaranteed crowd-pleaser, even for the pickiest eaters.

The secret to this dish lies in its simplicity. We're using readily available ingredients and straightforward techniques to create a meal that's both satisfying and impressive. Don't be fooled by the short ingredient list – each component plays a vital role in building the overall flavor profile. From the golden-brown chicken to the fragrant garlic and the rich cream sauce, every bite is an explosion of deliciousness.

One of the best things about this recipe is its versatility. Feel free to customize it to your liking. Add some chopped vegetables like spinach, broccoli, or bell peppers for extra nutrients and texture. You can also experiment with different types of pasta – penne, fettuccine, or even rotini would work perfectly. And for a little extra kick, try adding a pinch of red pepper flakes to the sauce.

Choosing the right pasta is key. While any pasta will technically work, I highly recommend using a long pasta shape like spaghetti or linguine. These shapes allow the creamy sauce to cling to every strand, ensuring a perfect bite every time. When cooking the pasta, be sure to cook it al dente – slightly firm to the bite – as it will continue to cook slightly in the sauce.

To ensure the chicken is cooked through and juicy, make sure to cut it into even-sized pieces before cooking. This will help it cook evenly and prevent it from drying out. I like to use chicken breast for this recipe, but you can also use chicken thighs for a richer flavor. Remember to season the chicken generously with salt, pepper, and any other spices you enjoy.

The creamy garlic sauce is what truly makes this dish special. The key is to cook the garlic gently over low heat to avoid burning it. Burnt garlic can have a bitter taste, which will ruin the flavor of the sauce. Once the garlic is fragrant, add the cream and Parmesan cheese and simmer until the sauce thickens slightly. Be careful not to boil the sauce, as this can cause it to separate.

Garnish your Chicken Garlic Pasta with fresh parsley or basil for a pop of color and freshness. Serve immediately and enjoy! This recipe is also great for meal prepping. Simply store the pasta and sauce separately and combine them when you're ready to eat. Reheat gently over low heat or in the microwave.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pasta (spaghetti or linguine recommended)
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley or basil, for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: Red pepper flakes, to taste
  • Optional: 1 cup chopped vegetables (spinach, broccoli, bell peppers)

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions until al dente. Reserve about 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. While pasta is cooking, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken and cook until golden brown and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Add minced garlic to the skillet and cook for 1 minute, or until fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic.
  4. Pour in heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce slightly thickens.
  5. Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using).
  6. Add cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat in the sauce. Add reserved pasta water if needed to adjust the consistency of the sauce.
  7. Stir in chopped vegetables (if using).
  8. Serve immediately, garnished with fresh parsley or basil.
