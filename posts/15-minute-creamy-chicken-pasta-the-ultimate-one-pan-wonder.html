15-Minute Creamy Chicken Pasta: The Ultimate One-Pan Wonder!

Pasta & Quick Meals 1/11/2026

Life gets busy, and sometimes the last thing you want to do is spend hours in the kitchen. That's where this 15-Minute Creamy Chicken Pasta comes to the rescue! It's the perfect weeknight meal when you're short on time but don't want to sacrifice flavor or quality. This recipe is a game-changer because it all cooks in one pan, meaning less cleanup and more time to relax.

The beauty of this dish lies in its simplicity. We're talking tender chicken, perfectly cooked pasta, and a luscious, creamy sauce that will tantalize your taste buds. The secret? Cooking the pasta directly in the sauce! This allows the pasta to absorb all those delicious flavors, resulting in a truly unforgettable dish. Don't be intimidated by the one-pan cooking method; it's surprisingly easy and efficient.

While the recipe is straightforward, feel free to customize it to your liking. Want to add some veggies? Spinach, broccoli, or mushrooms would be excellent additions. Prefer a spicier kick? Add a pinch of red pepper flakes or a dash of hot sauce. The possibilities are endless! The key is to have fun and experiment with flavors that you enjoy.

Choosing the right pasta is also important. I recommend using a smaller pasta shape like penne, rotini, or farfalle. These shapes cook quickly and evenly in the one-pan method. Avoid long pasta shapes like spaghetti or linguine, as they can be more difficult to manage in the pan.

The creamy sauce is what truly elevates this dish. We're using a combination of chicken broth, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese to create a rich and decadent sauce that coats every strand of pasta and piece of chicken. Don't skimp on the Parmesan – it adds a salty, savory flavor that complements the other ingredients perfectly.

Worried about overcooking the chicken? Don't be! Cutting the chicken into small, bite-sized pieces ensures that it cooks quickly and evenly. Just make sure the chicken is cooked through before adding the pasta. A meat thermometer is always a good idea to ensure proper doneness. The internal temperature should reach 165°F (74°C).

So, ditch the takeout and whip up this 15-Minute Creamy Chicken Pasta tonight! It's a guaranteed crowd-pleaser that will become a regular in your weeknight dinner rotation. Prepare to be amazed by how quickly and easily you can create a restaurant-quality meal at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 8 ounces pasta (penne, rotini, or farfalle)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and season with Italian seasoning, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Cook until chicken is browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.
  2. Pour in chicken broth and bring to a boil. Add pasta and stir to combine.
  3. Reduce heat to medium, cover, and simmer for 8-10 minutes, or until pasta is cooked al dente and most of the liquid has been absorbed. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  4. Stir in heavy cream and Parmesan cheese until sauce is smooth and creamy.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley (optional) and serve immediately.
